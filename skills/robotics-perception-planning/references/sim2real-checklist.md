# Sim To Real Checklist

Check these when real-robot results trail simulation.

1. sensor calibration and timestamp alignment
2. frame conventions and transform chains
3. actuator limits and delays
4. environment mismatch
5. contact or dynamics model mismatch
6. localization drift
7. safety layers masking controller behavior
8. reset procedure differences
9. data preprocessing mismatch
10. operator intervention hidden in real runs
